JD+A Custom Builders

Website Design and Brand Foundation Proposal

A phased approach to clarifying the JD+A brand, strengthening visibility, and supporting long-term growth.

Overview

JD+A Custom Builders creates truly custom homes and spaces with a high level of care, flexibility, and craftsmanship. The goal of this engagement is to ensure the JD+A brand visually represents the same quality and attention to detail as each of their projects.

Through thoughtful strategic design, we will clarify JD+A's story, process, and client experience in a clear and compelling way. Using the website and supporting materials, potential clients will be able to see themselves in the work and understand the building process before reaching out.

This work focuses on clearly communicating how JD+A operates, what clients can expect, and what makes the JD+A experience different from other builders.

1
Phase 1

Brand and Process Clarification

The first phase establishes a clear, client-focused articulation of how JD+A works and what differentiates the company from other builders.

This work informs the website structure, future marketing, and how JD+A consistently presents itself across all channels.

Scope Includes

  • Review of existing website, content, imagery, and messaging
  • Conversations with JD and Sandra to capture company history and approach
  • Definition of JD+A's custom-first approach, emphasizing flexibility and collaboration
  • Development of plain-language descriptions of the building process centered on the client experience
  • Identification of core messages that remain consistent across website and marketing materials
  • Creation of a clear narrative framework that supports current and future content

Deliverables

  • Written client-facing process narrative
  • Core messaging and positioning notes
  • Content outline for website and future marketing materials
Investment
$2,000
2
Phase 2

Website Restructure and Redesign

This phase reorganizes and redesigns the website to improve usability, clarity, and engagement. The goal is to make it easier for potential clients to understand JD+A's process, navigate the site, and see themselves in the work.

The site will prioritize the client journey while still showcasing craftsmanship, project details, and the collaborative building experience.

Scope Includes

  • Audit of current site structure, content, and imagery
  • Redefinition of page hierarchy, navigation, and user flow
  • Redesign of the homepage and key interior pages with emphasis on storytelling and photography
  • Content planning and layout design that can complement platforms such as Houzz
  • Development of content placement and microcopy to guide users
  • Preparation for Sanity CMS implementation as a headless solution

Deliverables

  • Page structure and content mapping documentation
  • Homepage and key interior page design and content strategy
  • Content placement guidance and microcopy
  • Headless CMS implementation using Sanity for flexible content management
Investment
$4,500
3
Phase 3

Media Strategy & Authority Positioning

With the brand foundation and website clarified, the next step is shaping how JD+A is perceived in the broader market.

For a custom builder, growth does not come from volume marketing. It comes from credibility, referrals, and trusted visibility. This phase defines how JD+A builds long-term authority — not just attention.

Rather than focusing narrowly on social media posts, this work creates a structured media strategy that increases recognition, strengthens third-party validation, and keeps JD+A top of mind among past and future clients.

Strategic Objectives

  • Elevate JD+A's perceived authority in the custom home market
  • Increase visibility in places high-end homeowners seek inspiration
  • Create repeatable systems for project promotion and submission
  • Support referral momentum and reputation growth
  • Maintain consistent visibility without overextending internal resources

Core Components

Earned Media & Editorial Strategy: Target regional and national design publications with curated project narratives and photography standards to secure editorial features.

Reputation & Referral Amplification: Develop testimonial collection frameworks, case study formatting, and review management systems to strengthen referral momentum.

Social Media as Strategic Support: Establish content pillars, voice guidelines, and posting cadence that integrate with the website and amplify project visibility.

Deliverables

  • Comprehensive Media Strategy document
  • Target publication and exposure list
  • Project submission narrative template
  • Testimonial and review framework
  • Content pillar and voice guidelines
  • 60-day visibility roadmap
Investment
$1,500–$2,500
